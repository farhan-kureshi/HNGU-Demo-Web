About Us

Hemchandracharya North Gujarat University (HNGU) is one of the leading universities in Gujarat, established in 1986 in the historic city of Patan. With a commitment to academic
excellence, innovation, and inclusive growth, HNGU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and research programs across various disciplines.
