Courses We offer

We offer a wide range of courses across various disciplines.

Intermediate

LIntermediate (Higher Secondary Education): It is the stage of education after the 10th standard, usually 11th and 12th grades. Students choose streams like Science, Commerce, or Arts depending on their interests and future goals. This phase builds the foundation for higher studies.

Degree

After completing 12th grade, students can enroll in degree programs such as B.A. (Bachelor of Arts), B.Com. (Bachelor of Commerce), B.Sc. (Bachelor of Science), BBA, BCA, etc. These are typically 3-year programs and mark the beginning of higher education.

post Greduation

Post Graduation (PG / Master’s Degree): After completing a degree, students can pursue a Master’s program like M.A., M.Com., M.Sc., MBA, MCA, etc. These programs generally last 2 years and allow specialization in a particular subject or profession.
